1 cup butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
2 1/4 cups flour
2 cups chocolate chips

Soften butter.  Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.  Cream butter and sugars.  Add eggs and vanilla, mix well.
Add salt, baking soda, and flour, mix well.  Add chocolate chips, mix by hand.  Drop cookies by rounded
tablespoon onto cookie sheets.  Bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.  Let rest for 1 minute, then
transfer to cooling racks.  Makes 36-72 cookies.
